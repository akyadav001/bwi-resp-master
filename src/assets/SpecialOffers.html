Best Western Rewards Members residing in the United States, Canada, or the Caribbean will receive a Promotion Reward if they: (a) register for the Promotion and (b) complete two (2) separate qualified stays* between February 2, 2015 and March 15, 2015 at any Best Western® branded hotel worldwide.

    Members can register for this promotion online at www.bestwestern.com/spring, on property by speaking with a front desk clerk at any Best Western® branded hotel, or over the phone with a representative at Best Western International, Inc.’s reservation center.

    Members may choose one gift card reward from among the Promotion Rewards shown below:

    • $35 Best Western Travel Card® Gift
    • $25 Amazon® Gift Card
    • $25 Best Buy® Gift Card
    • $25 iTunes® Gift Card
    • $25 Dunkin' Donuts® Gift Card

    For those Members registering for this Promotion on property or through the reservation center who do not select a specific gift card award from the Promotion Rewards shown above upon registering for this Promotion, they can visit www.bestwestern.com/spring at any time to make their selection. If, however, the Member has not made a specific gift card selection by one (1) week after the check-out for the second qualified stay, the gift card reward shall default to the Best Western Travel Card® for $35 USD. The Best Western Travel Card® for $35 USD expires July 31, 2015, with no extensions, substitutions or replacements available, and it may not be used in conjunction with any full prepaid rates (e.g., WEB SPECIAL, BEST VALUE, et cetera). All Best Western Travel Card terms and conditions apply (visit www.travelcard.bestwestern.com for such terms and conditions).

    Members must provide their Best Western Rewards® account number at the time of booking and have a valid email address in their Best Western Rewards member account profile. Best Western Rewards enrollment (existing or new) is required prior to the first qualified stay. Best Western Rewards enrollment may be completed by visiting www.bestwesternrewards.com.
