TRIPADVISOR CERTIFICATE OF EXCELLENCE WINNERS

TripAdvisor, the world's largest travel site, recently awarded its 2013 Certificate of Excellence Awards to highly rated accommodations, restaurants and attractions worldwide.

More than 1,500 Best Western hotels were honored with the accolade. Only 10% of accommodations listed on TripAdvisor receive the prestigious award.

The Certificate of Excellence Award honors hospitality excellence, and is given only to establishments that consistently achieve outstanding traveler reviews on TripAdvisor. To qualify, businesses must maintain an overall rating of four or higher, out of a possible five, as reviewed by travelers on TripAdvisor.

For those Best Western hotels that have received the TripAdvisor Certificate of Excellence, a banner signifying the honor will be featured on the property's Hotel Amenities page.

Best Western is honored that so many of our hotels have earned the TripAdvisor Certificate of Excellence.
