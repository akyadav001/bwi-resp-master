Groups Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most commonly asked questions about Best Western International's groups programs. If you do not find the answers you're looking for, please contact us for further assistance.

  • Submitting a Group Request

    How do I request a group reservation?

    The Group RFP (Request for Proposal) is a convenient electronic form allowing you to customize your request for group rates for 4 or more rooms. The RFP form will allow you to specify exactly what your group needs and receive competitive rates based on those requirements. Our Group RFP allows you to customize the elements specific to each group. Our easy step by step procedure will guide you through the process of selecting a hotel for your group booking, entering in your requirements such as contact information and date and then linking you to our online electronic form to submit your RFP.

    Which RFP (Request for Proposal) option should I use to request a meeting room with guest rooms?

    To request meeting space with guest rooms for 4 or more hotel rooms (with an alternate hotel choice), select the Book Groups & Meetings page to begin our easy step by step process.

  • Book Meeting Space Only

    How do I request meeting space only?

    To request meeting space click Groups & Meeting from the Group Travel Home page.

  • Book Group Sports Travel

    How do I submit a group request for Sports Travel?

    Click the Home Team Advantage link from the Group Travel Home page.

  • 1-3 Rooms - Submitting a Group Request(s)

    How do I submit a request to book 1-3 rooms without meeting space?

    Select the Book 1-3 rooms link from the Groups Home page.

    How do I submit a request to book 1-3 rooms with meeting space?

    To request meeting space with guest rooms click Groups & Meeting from the Group Travel Home page.

  • Response Time

    How long will it take to receive an answer to my Group Request(s)?

    Best Western understands that all groups are not alike and because of this, each group will require different elements to make their hotel stay comfortable. Your group request will sent to all requested hotels. Hotels will respond directly to you via email within 1-2 business days.

  • Canceling/Change a Group Reservation(s)

    How do I cancel or change a group reservation?

    Any modifications or cancellations of your group reservations must be communicated directly to the hotel before or on the date stated on your booking confirmation. Refer to your booking confirmation for the phone number and sales contact.
